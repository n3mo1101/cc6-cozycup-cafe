About Us


Discover the story behind CozyCup Café


CozyCup Café is known as the most comfy café all over the world. We start to build on August 14, 2025. We began as a small business focused on creating simple coffee drinks such as Americano, iced café latte, matcha coffee, and iced milk cappuccino. We have a social media platform for delivery and taking orders online; we are starting to add new styles and flavors and upgrade the coffees.
